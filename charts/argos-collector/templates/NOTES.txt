  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} rollout status deployment/{{ include "argos-collector.fullname" . }}

The cluster row appears in argosd within one polling interval. Confirm it
landed:

  curl -H "Authorization: Bearer <admin-pat>" \
    {{ .Values.serverURL }}/v1/clusters | jq '.items[] | select(.name=="{{ .Values.clusterName }}")'
